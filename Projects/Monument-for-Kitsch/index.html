An Installation with a concrete cornucopia that plays a deconstructed steelpan sound (made by Jason Fitzroy Jeffers.) Often tropical aesthetics are seen by western societies as ironic or kitsch. It plays with the question who can decide what is kitsch and what is not? The cornucopia lights the price tag on a golden fruit, emphasising this question.
